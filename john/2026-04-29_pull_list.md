## Search tips

- **CountyFusion (LC)**: pulls from `BOUNDS J H`, `BOUNDS M S`, `BOUNDS-MARTIN`, etc. all work. Older Book-letter records (Books A–S) sometimes have digitization gaps — if not found by name search, try volume/page directly.
- **GovOS (FC)**: coverage is thinner pre-1925; for older volumes (Vol. 45 1913, Vol. 42 1913, Vol. 85 1925) verify whether the volume is in the online index before assuming a missing record means the instrument doesn't exist.
- **Probate / court files**: neither county's probate index is online in any meaningful depth. Email or call the county clerk with the name + estimated death year + "probate file request."
- **Highest-leverage FC trip**: a single Fairfield courthouse day could knock out the J.M. Bounds 1915 probate, J.V. Bounds Jr. probate (No. 3217), Case 8153-B partition judgment, and FC Vol. 45 Pg. 128 — four medium/high items.
